    Ek main hi toh hu apne sath


    Categories: Story Telling
    Word count: 287
    Written by Samdisha on 14 Jan 2022


    aaj khud se rubaru hui
    kafi mahine ho gaye the khud se mulakat hue
    bhot si kahaniya kisse the jo janne ko mile
    pta chala ki iss duniya ki bhag dorh mei itni mashgul ho gyi thi, ki bhul hi gyi thi mai bhi hu
    kafi der baate krti rhi
    kabhi hasi toh kabhi roi
    bhot yaad aati h yr teri, khud ne khud se bola
    teri vo faltu baato ki
    teri vo bevajh hasne kiv teri vo tension free rehne ki
    bhot yaad aati h
    kaise tu kabhi fikr hi nhi krti thi kisi cheez ki
    aur ab toh ek ek cheez ko hazaro baar sochti h
    kaise tu bindass bolti thi, jo mn mei aata tha keh deti thi
    aur ab hazaro raaz chupaye hue h zehn mei
    kaise pehle pata hi na tha dar kya hota h
    aur ab dar mei jeeti h
    tu badl gyi h yaar, andr se awaaz aayi
    Ye sb sunke jhatka sa lga
    pr ek cheez smjh aayi, bhot kuch Miss kr diya yr
    Khud ko bhool bethi thi
    Tabhi toh itna badal gyi thi ki pehchan hi na Pau ki mai hu
    aaj smjh aaya, ki khud se badhiya dost na milega
    Isiliye phir apne aap se kaha
    sorry yr maaf krde
    aur vada kiya
    ki ab se roz milungi
    khud ko aur janungi
    ab yaar jo rutha h usse mnana h
    Kyunki ek mai hi toh hun apne saath
    Hmesha hr pal hr khushi mei hr gm mei mai hi toh hu apne saath
